Noble Rock Orchard

Dendrobium nobile (Noble Rock Orchid) is an epiphytic or lithophytic orchid boasting short racemes of 2-4 sweet-scented, rose-pink flowers, up to 2 in. across (5 cm), adorned with a dark purple lip..

Showy Tiger Orchid

The Queen of Orchids, the tiger orchid (Grammatophyllum speciosum) is the world largest orchid by plant height and weight, with each plant growing in a huge clump that can weigh up to 2 tonnes and is composed of thick, cane-like stems that can reach up to 7.6m in height..

Tiger Stripe Orchid

This species has grasslike leaf blades with a yellowish base and produces between 4 and 7 long-lasting flowers. The flowers are yellow with brown-striped petals that are spreading. The margin is frilly with large, irregular red spots.
